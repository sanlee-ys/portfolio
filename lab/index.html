Learning log

I came from infra and backend, so the front-end here is me climbing the UI envelope in the open, one technique at a time. Each lesson captures the concept, the why, and the files that changed. They render on GitHub:

  • Scroll-reveal: IntersectionObserver, CSS transitions, prefers-reduced-motion.
  • Hover micro-interactions + animated gradient: @keyframes, pseudo-elements, z-index stacking.
  • Layout: Flexbox & Grid: 1D vs 2D, auto-fit/minmax, fr units.
  • Robust & fluid: :focus-visible, clamp(), container queries.
  • Scroll-driven storytelling: scroll/view timelines, position: sticky, progressive enhancement.
  • Data-driven SVG diagram: viewBox, createElementNS, keyboard-operable SVG.
  • The domain: DNS, CNAMEs & email: A vs CNAME, apex rules, MX, receive-only forwarding.
